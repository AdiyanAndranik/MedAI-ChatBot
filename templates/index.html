About MedAI

MedAI is a specialized medical AI assistant designed to provide accurate and empathetic answers to your health-related questions. Powered by advanced language models and medical knowledge, MedAI can help you understand symptoms, explore treatment options, and learn about health conditions.

How to Use MedAI

  • Ask clear, specific questions about symptoms, conditions, or treatments.
  • Examples: "What causes migraines?" or "How do I manage a sprained ankle?"
  • MedAI provides informational responses, not medical diagnoses. Always consult a healthcare professional for serious concerns.
  • Use the chat interface to interact with MedAI in real-time.

Important Notes

MedAI is not a doctor. Its responses are for educational purposes only. For medical emergencies or personalized advice, contact a licensed healthcare provider.
